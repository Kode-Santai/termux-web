Response Times

Since Termux is maintained by a dedicated community of volunteers, response times may vary:

  • Bug Reports (GitHub): Usually addressed within 1-7 days
  • Community Chat (Gitter): Usually get responses within hours
  • Reddit (r/termux): Active community, often get answers quickly
  • Contact Form: We'll respond within 5-10 business days
